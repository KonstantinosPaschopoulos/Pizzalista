Welcome to Pizzalista—your go-to platform for discovering and sharing everything about pizza. Whether you're a casual pizza fan or a connoisseur on the hunt for the best slice, our app is designed to bring pizza lovers together in a fun, social, and engaging way.

We believe pizza is more than just food—it’s a cultural experience that brings people together. That’s why we built Pizzalista, a platform where everyone from the casual eater to the seasoned critic can find their passion. We want to be the go-to social network for pizza lovers, helping people discover, rate, and enjoy the best pizza around the world.

At Pizzalista, you can enhance your pizza experience by:

Creating a Wishlist: Save pizzerias you want to visit in one easy-to-access list, so you’re always ready for your next pizza adventure.

Rating Pizzerias: Share your thoughts by scoring your pizza experiences.

Logging your Visits: Keep track of every pizzeria you've visited.

Saving your Favourites: Save your favorite pizzerias for easy access later.

Join us and become part of a community of pizza lovers!

This website is made with lots of love (for pizza) from Konstantinos Paschopoulos.
